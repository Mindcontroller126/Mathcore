What is a Linear Equation?

A linear equation is an algebraic equation where each term is either a constant or the product of a constant and a single variable. Linear equations appear as straight lines when graphed and follow the general form:

ax + b = c

Where a, b, and c are constants, and x is the variable we're solving for. The goal is to isolate the variable on one side of the equation.

Key Concept: Whatever operation you perform on one side of the equation, you must perform on the other side to maintain equality.

Step-by-Step Solving Method

To solve linear equations, follow these systematic steps:

1. Simplify Both Sides

Combine like terms and eliminate parentheses using the distributive property.

2. Move Variable Terms to One Side

Add or subtract terms to get all variables on one side of the equation.

3. Move Constant Terms to the Other Side

Add or subtract to isolate the variable term.

4. Isolate the Variable

Divide or multiply to get the variable by itself.

5. Check Your Solution

Substitute your answer back into the original equation to verify it works.

Worked Examples

Example 1: Basic Linear Equation

Solve: 3x + 7 = 22

Step 1: Subtract 7 from both sides
3x + 7 - 7 = 22 - 7
3x = 15
Step 2: Divide both sides by 3

x = 5
Step 3: Check the solution

Example 2: Equation with Variables on Both Sides

Solve: 5x - 3 = 2x + 9

Step 1: Subtract 2x from both sides
5x - 2x - 3 = 2x - 2x + 9
3x - 3 = 9
Step 2: Add 3 to both sides
3x - 3 + 3 = 9 + 3
3x = 12
Step 3: Divide both sides by 3
x = 4

Example 3: Equation with Fractions

Solve: (x/2) + 3 = 7

Step 1: Subtract 3 from both sides
x/2 = 4
Step 2: Multiply both sides by 2
x = 8
Common Mistake: Don't forget to distribute negative signs when simplifying! For example: -(2x - 3) becomes -2x + 3, not -2x - 3.

Practice Problems

Problem 1

Solve: 2x + 5 = 17

Solution:

2x + 5 = 17

2x = 12 (subtract 5 from both sides)

x = 6 (divide both sides by 2)

Problem 2

Solve: 4x - 7 = 3x + 2

Solution:

4x - 7 = 3x + 2

x - 7 = 2 (subtract 3x from both sides)

x = 9 (add 7 to both sides)

Problem 3

Solve: -3(x - 4) = 18

Solution:

-3(x - 4) = 18

-3x + 12 = 18 (distribute -3)

-3x = 6 (subtract 12)

x = -2 (divide by -3)

Problem 4

Solve: (x/4) + 2 = 5

Solution:

x/4 + 2 = 5

x/4 = 3 (subtract 2)

x = 12 (multiply by 4)
